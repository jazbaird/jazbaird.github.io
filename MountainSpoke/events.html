Cycle the Continental Divide

From Bozeman to Billings, this grand adventure takes you to mountain peaks, delicious valleys, across rivers and streams, for the ride of your life! Test your skills with this technical, 6-day ride.

Route: Bozeman, Canyon Mountain, Livingston Peak, Mt. Rae, Natural Bridge, Boulder River, Sugarloaf Mountain, Stillwater River, Yellowstone River, Billings
Cost: $1849 (includes meals, lodging)
Basic Requirements: Must be 18 yrs. Must complete the medical form and waiver.
